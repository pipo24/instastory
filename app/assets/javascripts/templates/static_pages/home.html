Welcome to InstaStory

With this app you can save and view images for any Trip, Event or Adventure that you experienced by taking Instagram pictures by a specific hashtag and collecting them into a Story for users to enjoy.

InstaStory is set to revolutionize the way that Instagram works for the better so that users can not just save images but also organize them by tags and create albums that friends and family can use to experience their adventures.

This is the first version of the website, still to come in version 2 is the ability to display images in a map view to follow a route taken in a trip.
